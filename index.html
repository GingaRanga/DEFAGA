About the Gun Club

Our Indoor Range

We have a 20 yard range with 4 firing positions. It accomodates all traditional handgun calibers as well as .22 rimfire rifles. The range is properly ventilated and provincially approved. A qualified range officer will always be present to supervise the safe use of your firearm and range.

What We Do

We are primarily a pistol and rifle club for both traditional and practical target shooting. We welcome all new members seeking a new and challenging sport. You may join us in a two day trial period before making the decision to join the club. We will be surprised if you do not become hooked!

When We Meet

Traditional pistol "Bullseye" shooting is held Monday evenings, 7:00 pm to 9:30 pm and "Practical Pistol" is on Wednesdays, 7:00 pm to 9:00 pm. A Practical Rifle Section will be starting soon. If you would like to visit please come see us on a Monday night. Wednesday nights are for regular members only. Our contact information is on the top of this page. Hope to see you soon!
